Plan Features

LIC Jeevan Labh - Key Features

A limited premium paying term endowment plan with high maturity benefits

Limited Premium Payment

Pay premiums for 10, 15, or 16 years and enjoy coverage for longer period.

High Bonus Rates

Get Simple Reversionary Bonus (SRB) of ₹40-49 per ₹1,000 sum assured based on SA slab.

Death Benefit

Sum Assured + Accrued Bonus + Final Additional Bonus (if any) to nominee.

Maturity Benefit

Sum Assured + Accrued Bonus + Final Additional Bonus at policy maturity.

Loan Facility

Avail loan against your policy after paying premiums for 2 consecutive years.

Tax Benefits

Premium qualifies for deduction under Section 80C. Maturity proceeds tax-free under Section 10(10D).

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

LIC Jeevan Labh (Plan 736) is a limited premium payment endowment plan that offers high bonus rates and combines life cover with savings. Pay premiums for 10, 15, or 16 years and get life protection plus maturity benefits.

LIC Jeevan Labh bonus rates for 2025: ₹40-47 per ₹1,000 SA (below ₹5L), ₹41-48 (₹5L-10L), ₹42-49 (₹10L+). This is one of the highest bonus rates among LIC plans.

Maturity benefit = Sum Assured + Accrued Simple Reversionary Bonus + Final Additional Bonus (if any). With high bonus rates, the total maturity value can be significantly higher than the sum assured.

Yes, you can avail loan against your Jeevan Labh policy after paying premiums for 2 consecutive years. Loan amount depends on surrender value.
