LIC Smart Pension - Key Features

A comprehensive retirement solution with flexible annuity options

Lifetime Pension

Receive guaranteed pension for life. The annuity continues as long as you live.

Joint Life Option

Continue pension to spouse after your demise. Multiple options available.

Inflation Protection

Increasing annuity option to protect against inflation. Pension increases annually.

Multiple Payout Modes

Choose monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or yearly pension payouts.

Return of Purchase Price

On death (if opted), get back the purchase price paid for the annuity.

Tax Benefits

Premium qualifies for deduction under Section 80C. Pension is taxable as per income slab.

The minimum entry age is 40 years and maximum is 100 years. There is no maximum age limit for purchasing the policy.
