IRDAI Compliance 2026

Regulatory Compliance & Customer Benefits

All Star Health Insurance policies are fully compliant with IRDAI Health Insurance Regulations 2024 and the Master Circular on Health Insurance Business (May 2024). Key regulatory benefits include:

Max PED Waiting: 36 Months

Pre-existing diseases covered after maximum 36 months (down from 48 months)

Moratorium: 5 Years

After 5 years continuous coverage, claims cannot be repudiated except fraud

No Maximum Entry Age

Insurers cannot deny coverage solely based on age - lifelong coverage

Lifetime Renewability

Mandatory for all health insurance products - renew for life

Fast Cashless Claims

Pre-authorization: 1 hour | Final discharge: 3 hours | Insurer bears extra costs

Senior Premium Cap

Annual premium increase capped at 10% for ages 60+ (unless IRDAI approves higher)

GST Exemption: Individual/retail health insurance premiums are GST-exempt from September 22, 2025. Portability: Waiting period credits and no-claim bonuses transfer when switching insurers. Cashless Everywhere: Cashless treatment allowed at non-network hospitals with prior intimation.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Star Health's Claim Settlement Ratio in 2026?

Star Health's average claim settlement ratio (CSR) for 2022-2025 stands at approximately 85%. The company settled over 1.28 crore claims worth ₹61,586 crore since inception. 96% of cashless approvals are issued within 3 hours.

Which Star Health policy is best for families in 2026?

Family Health Optima is ideal for families with affordable premiums, maternity cover, and automatic restoration. For comprehensive coverage, Super Star offers unlimited sum insured with 21 add-ons. Star Comprehensive provides no room rent capping with high sum insured options.

Does Star Health cover pre-existing diseases for senior citizens?

Yes! Senior Citizens Red Carpet policy covers pre-existing diseases from Day 1 with no medical screening required. Entry age is 60-75 years with lifelong renewability. Premium increase capped at 10% annually as per IRDAI regulations.

What is the waiting period for pre-existing diseases?

As per IRDAI 2024 regulations, maximum waiting period for pre-existing diseases is 36 months (reduced from 48 months). Star Health offers buy-back PED options to reduce waiting period. Moratorium period is 5 years after which claims cannot be repudiated except for fraud.

How many hospitals are in Star Health's cashless network?

Star Health has 14,000+ cashless hospitals - the largest hospital network in India. You can also avail Cashless Everywhere at non-network hospitals with prior intimation (48 hours planned, 24 hours emergency).

Is GST applicable on Star Health Insurance premiums?

No GST is applicable on individual/retail health insurance premiums from September 22, 2025. This makes health insurance more affordable. Senior citizen premium increases are capped at 10% annually as per IRDAI.
